Object-fit

Object-fit is a property for images that specifies how the image should be rezised to fit its container

Values object-fit property can take:

  • fill - This is the default value. The image fills its container. Streches or squishes the image if neccesary
  • contain - Prevents the image from streching or squishing. Rezises to fill the container
  • cover - Same as contain, but the image will be clipped off to fit
  • none - The image will not be rezised
  • scale-down - The image will take on the smaller aspect ratio of the two values: none or contain

Object-position

Put simply object-position is used to position an image inside its container.

Object-position takes x/y coordinates as its values.
